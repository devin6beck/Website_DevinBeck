Hi, I'm Devin Beck

About Me

Originally from Arizona, I've made Taos, New Mexico my home since 2012. For a decade, I embraced the outdoors, spending my summers as a river guide and my winters snowboarding and working as a sales supervisor at Taos Ski Valley. However, my passion for technology led me to a recent transition into a full-time IT professional role at Taos Ski Valley, though I still guide on the weekends. Outside of learning a new function or scouting a rapid, you can find me snowboarding at TSV, floating on my Onewheel, or undertaking a project with my wife.

My Wife

A big shout-out to my incredible wife, Hannah Beck, who is the proud owner of Taos Best Cleaning. Together, we've explored mountains and rivers, building a beautiful life in Taos with our two dogs and cat.

My Career

My journey has been a blend of diverse experiences. From navigating the outdoors to diving into the ever-evolving world of IT, I am now focusing on expanding my development skills.
