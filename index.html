About Me

I’ve lived in Taos since 2012. I spent many years guiding rivers in the summer and snowboarding in the winter. Now I work as the Revenue Auditor at Taos Ski Valley, where I build and refine systems that make financial operations smoother and more accurate. I still guide when the season allows.

I live here with my wife, Hannah, owner of Taos Best Cleaning, and our small pack of animals. We spend our time snowboarding, onewheeling, rafting, and enjoying our cozy Taos house.

Career

My work centers on revenue auditing, automation, and process improvement. I build workflows that reduce repetitive manual entry and help teams move faster with greater accuracy. Tools I use often include VBA, Excel, Power Automate, and Power Query. I like taking something messy and turning it into something that makes sense.
