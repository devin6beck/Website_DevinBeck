About Me

My name is Devin Beck. Originally from Arizona, I've made Taos, New Mexico my home since 2012.

For a decade, I embraced the outdoors, spending my summers as a river guide and my winters snowboarding and working as a sales supervisor at Taos Ski Valley. However, my passion for technology led me to a recent transition into a full-time IT professional role at Taos Ski Valley, even though I still guide on the weekends.

My Wife and Our Story

Life's adventures are even better with my incredible wife, Hannah, by my side. Together, we've explored mountains and rivers, building a beautiful life in Taos. She's the proud owner of Taos Best Cleaning.

My Career

My career journey has been an intriguing blend of diverse experiences. From navigating the thrilling outdoors to diving into the ever-evolving world of IT, I am now focusing on expanding my development skills.
